Connecting Global Youth

Growing up, I discovered the most valuable connections weren't built across digital platforms, but around the soft warmth of a shared crackling campfire. This realization inspired me to build the foundations of Frog Toad Lake.

My strategy focuses on creating safe, cozy ecosystems where young travelers from 45+ nations learn about their neighbors while navigating wild territories.

"Nature is the ultimate, non-judgmental classroom. When you strip away notifications, real communication and character emerge organically."

Ethan Hawke

Preparedness is Inner Peace

I've spent over a decade leading deep-country rescue patrols. Teaching survival isn't about fostering panic — it's about cultivating quiet, unwavering confidence in any condition.

At Frog Toad Lake I work with campers to identify forest flora, map terrain using stars, and design windproof shelters.

"True bushcraft is not about fighting the forest; it is about learning its language so you can rest comfortably in its heart."

Maya Lin

The Joy of Team Play

I coordinate everything that moves, swims, runs, and cheers at the lake. I design water safety programs and team field games that build fitness alongside deep collaborative trust.

Whether organizing lake regattas or forest hide-and-seek, my focus is ensuring every camper feels the thrill of shared victory.

"You can learn more about a teammate during an hour of outdoor games than in a lifetime of standard conversation. Play breaks all barriers."
